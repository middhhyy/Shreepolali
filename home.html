ABOUT US

Mangaluru's Premier Interior Studio

Shree Polali Interiors has been transforming homes and commercial spaces across coastal Karnataka since 2014. We combine traditional Tulu craftsmanship with contemporary global aesthetics to create spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

Based at Padavinangadi, Pachanady — our workshop and design studio serves clients across Mangaluru and beyond with passion, precision, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

MEET THE VISIONARY

Bharathesh Bhandary

FOUNDER · PRINCIPAL DESIGNER · MANGALURU

With over a decade of experience transforming homes and commercial spaces across coastal Karnataka, Bharathesh Bhandary is the creative force behind every project at Sri Polali Interiors. His journey began with a singular belief: that world-class interior design should be accessible to every discerning homeowner.

Trained in design principles and material science, Bharathesh combines traditional Tulu craftsmanship with contemporary global aesthetics — personally overseeing each project from the first sketch to the final finishing touch.

"I don't just design rooms. I craft the backdrop for your life's most precious moments."
